GREEN JUNE - WORLD ENVIRONMENT

Green June is the moth of environment awareness established in Brazil to promote the preservation of the environment and the adoption of sustainsble practices. During this period, schools, companies and communities carry out activities to encourage the protection of nature, such as tree planting, recycling and the responsible use of naturalresources

The movie The Lorax is often remembered during this campaign because it shows, in a fun and emotional way, how the destruction of trees can affect all life around us.

Just like in the story, Green June reinforces that small daily actions such as saving water and protecting green ares make a big difference in ensuring a more sustainable future for everyone.
